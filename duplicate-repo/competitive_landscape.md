# Competitive Landscape

## What makes Focus Bear different from these apps?

Focus Bear is different because it combines several productivity features in one system. While many apps mainly focus on blocking distractions, Focus Bear also helps users build healthy routines and habits. It guides users through structured focus sessions and encourages positive behaviors such as taking breaks and following a daily routine. This makes it feel more like a productivity system rather than just a website blocker.

## If you were a user, why would you choose Focus Bear over competitors?

If I were a user, I might choose Focus Bear because it integrates multiple productivity tools together. Instead of using separate apps for blocking distractions, managing routines, and tracking habits, Focus Bear combines these features into one platform. I also like the idea that it focuses on helping users build long-term habits rather than only restricting access to distracting websites.

## What’s one feature that other apps have that Focus Bear doesn’t?

One feature that some competitors have is deeper customization and integrations with other productivity tools. For example, some apps allow integrations with calendars, task managers, or other productivity platforms, which can help users connect their workflow more easily.

## Based on your research, what’s one improvement you think Focus Bear could make?

One improvement Focus Bear could make is adding more integrations with other tools such as Google Calendar or task management apps. This could make it easier for users to connect their daily schedule with their focus sessions and routines, making the app more flexible for different workflows.
