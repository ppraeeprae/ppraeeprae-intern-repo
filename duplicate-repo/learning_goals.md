# Learning Goals

## What do you hope to learn from this internship?
I hope to gain real-world experience working in a professional development team. I want to learn how a tech company operates, how projects are managed, and how team members collaborate to build and improve products.

## What skills are you most excited to develop?
I am most excited to develop my programming and problem-solving skills. I also want to improve my experience with Git, version control, and working on real projects with a team.

## What do you think you can contribute to the team?
I believe I can contribute by being responsible, motivated, and willing to learn. I am happy to help with tasks, test features, and share ideas when working on projects.

## What is one area you feel less confident in that you’d like to improve?
I would like to improve my confidence in working with larger codebases and collaborating with other developers. I also want to become more comfortable asking questions and discussing technical ideas.

## What does success in this internship look like for you?
Success for me would mean gaining practical experience, improving my technical skills, and contributing meaningfully to the team’s projects. I would also like to finish the internship feeling more confident about working in the tech industry.
