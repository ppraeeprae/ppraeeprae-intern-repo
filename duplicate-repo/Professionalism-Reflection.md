# Professionalism at Focus Bear

## Research & Learn

### Professional vs Unprofessional behaviour in a casual work environment

Professional behaviour in a casual workplace means maintaining respect, responsibility, and clear communication even in a relaxed environment.

Examples of professional behaviour:
- Communicating respectfully and clearly with teammates.
- Being punctual and prepared for meetings.
- Listening to others’ ideas and collaborating effectively.
- Taking responsibility for tasks and following through on commitments.

Examples of unprofessional behaviour:
- Interrupting others during meetings.
- Ignoring messages or failing to respond to team communication.
- Speaking disrespectfully or dismissively to colleagues.
- Missing deadlines without explanation.

Even in a relaxed culture, professionalism ensures teamwork and productivity.


### Respectful communication in remote teams

Respectful communication in remote teams involves being mindful of tone and clarity in written communication.

Good practices include:
- Using clear and polite language in messages.
- Avoiding sarcasm or messages that could be misunderstood.
- Acknowledging messages and responding in a reasonable timeframe.
- Being open to different perspectives during discussions.

When disagreements occur, team members should remain respectful, focus on solving the problem, and avoid personal criticism.


### Giving and receiving feedback professionally

Professional feedback focuses on actions and improvement rather than personal criticism.

When giving feedback:
- Focus on specific behaviours or outcomes.
- Provide constructive suggestions for improvement.
- Be respectful and supportive.

When receiving feedback:
- Listen carefully without becoming defensive.
- Ask clarifying questions if needed.
- Use feedback as an opportunity to learn and improve.

Assuming positive intent helps maintain a healthy working relationship.

## Reflection

### Experience with good teamwork

In previous group projects at university, effective teamwork occurred when team members communicated regularly and respected each other's contributions.

Clear task allocation, regular updates, and mutual support helped ensure that everyone stayed aligned and completed their responsibilities. This created a positive environment where team members felt comfortable sharing ideas and asking for help.


## Steps to ensure clear and respectful communication

To maintain clear and inclusive communication I will:
- Be polite and professional in written and verbal communication.
- Confirm understanding of tasks and expectations.
- Respond to messages promptly.
- Be open to feedback and different perspectives.

---

### Creating a positive and professional work environment

I can contribute to a positive workplace by:
- Encouraging collaboration and teamwork.
- Respecting different opinions and backgrounds.
- Maintaining a positive attitude.
- Supporting colleagues when they need assistance.

A friendly and respectful environment helps everyone feel comfortable contributing ideas.


### Preparing for meetings with supervisors

To prepare for meetings I will:
- Review the project progress beforehand.
- Prepare questions or discussion points.
- Bring updates on completed work and current challenges.
- Take notes during meetings and follow up on action items.

Preparing in advance ensures meetings are productive and efficient.


### Being proactive during the internship

Being proactive means taking initiative rather than waiting for instructions.

I will:
- Research solutions before asking questions.
- Suggest ideas or improvements when appropriate.
- Provide regular updates on progress.
- Seek feedback to improve my work.

Focus Bear encourages interns to become increasingly self-led and take initiative in their work.


### Following up with colleagues or supervisors

If I need information or action from someone, I will:

1. Send a polite message or email explaining the request.
2. Wait a reasonable time for a response.
3. Send a follow-up reminder if necessary.

If the matter becomes urgent, I may escalate communication through other channels such as Discord, SMS, or a call depending on urgency.


### Things I will avoid

During my internship I will avoid:
- Ignoring messages or failing to provide updates.
- Coming to meetings unprepared.
- Asking questions that could easily be researched first.
- Ignoring feedback or repeating the same mistakes.

These behaviours can slow down the team and reduce productivity.
